Info

One player

The goal is to get as many points as possible.
Each wave there is a set of amount of balls you have to shoot until you can proceed.
With each wave the difficulty increases, but so does the amount points you gain which goes towards your score.
Until a ball has bounced on walls two times it cannot hit you.
If you get hit you lose.

Two player

The goal is to kill your opponent.
